other apps Company designates (the "**Apps**") — to Partner's audience, and Company will pay Partner a
share of the revenue generated by users Partner refers, on the terms below.

promotion complies with Section 6.

## 3. Company's Responsibilities
Company will: (a) provide the Apps and the promotional/attribution tools in Section 4 (e.g., referral
links and/or promo codes); (b) maintain reasonable tracking of referred users and revenue; and
(c) provide Partner a periodic report of attributed users and amounts owed.

## 5. Revenue Share
- **Rate:** Company will pay Partner **fifty percent (50%)** of **Net Revenue** (defined below)
  generated by users Partner refers via the Attribution Method during their subscription.
- **"Net Revenue"** means the amounts Company actually **receives** from those referred users for the
  Apps, **after** deducting: (a) Apple/Google app-store commissions and payment-processing fees;
  (b) refunds, chargebacks, and credits; and (c) applicable taxes. *(Note: "50% of profit" is
  ambiguous and hard to audit — this Agreement uses Net Revenue instead. If you truly mean net
  profit after server/marketing costs, define those costs here. Confirm which you want.)*
- **Duration of share:** Partner earns the share on a referred user's payments for **[choose: the
  first 12 months of that user's subscription · as long as that user remains subscribed]**.

## 6. Compliance, Disclosure & Conduct (important for health apps)
Partner agrees to:
- **Disclose the paid relationship** clearly and conspicuously wherever Partner promotes the Apps
  (e.g., "affiliate," "#ad," or "I earn a commission"), as required by the FTC and platform rules.
- Follow the rules of every platform used (Facebook group policies, App Store terms, etc.).
- **Not** make medical claims, guarantee health/weight outcomes, or state or imply the Apps provide
  medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The Apps are for tracking and education only.
- Not use spam, misleading statements, fake reviews, impersonation, or purchased/bot traffic. Traffic
  must be genuine.
- Not use Company's trademarks or brand-name drug marks (Ozempic®, Wegovy®, etc.) except as Company
  approves in writing.
Company may withhold payment for, and reverse attribution of, any traffic obtained in violation of
this Section.
