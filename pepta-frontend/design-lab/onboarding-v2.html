What changed from v2.0 (the "cheap" fixes): ① echo lines are now the same type scale as the question, dimmed to 50% — legible over the ground orbs while still clearly a "recall" under the full-ink question. ② Accent discipline: purple appears at most three times per screen (caret, one punctuation square, the sent/selected moment) — no gradients anywhere. ③ CTAs are quiet outlined pills; the only solid-purple button in the whole flow is the wall's "Start tracking". ④ The white is never flat: three huge 5%-purple orbs give the ground air. ⑤ Chip selection is neutral-ink (the app's own OptionCard convention), not purple. ⑥ The wall is near-black plum (#171128) with hairline value rows — the purple lives in ONE word and ONE button, not the whole screen. Mechanics unchanged: 32ms/char typing, tick/tap/thump haptic grammar, sent-bubble beat, 950ms auto-advance on single-select, Continue only for multi/wheels, 300ms fade + 14px rise, hairline progress + small back chevron.
